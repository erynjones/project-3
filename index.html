Fresh homemade treats everday!

We take pride in bringing you the “traditional” taste of homemade breads, pastries and cakes. We use only the best quality ingredients, combined skill and love of baking, to create fresh, light, melt-in-your-mouth delicacies. We love what we do and want to share it with you!

Our Products

Breads

There is an ample array of mouth-watering, light fluffy rolls, (whole wheat, diner, Kaiser), sliced bread (white, wheat, rye, raisin, cheese and cinnamon), and loaves (lemon, banana and cranberry orange). We also have breadcrumbs and croutons.

Cookies & Squares

We carry a variety of delectable sweets to satisfy the sweet tooth: Nanaimo bars, butter tart squares, and cookies (oatmeal, peanut butter, oatmeal raisin, shortbread, and sugar).

Pastries

Our selection of sumptuous, baked-from-scratch pastries includes apple fritters, doughnuts (Boston cream, maple glazed chocolate glazed, jelly–filled) and a variety of muffins (cranberry, chocolate hip, carrot, and lemon poppy seed).

Soups & Sandwhichs

If you are looking for a quick lunch, drop in for a hearty sandwich and homemade soup. Our soups and sandwiches are made fresh every morning and served piping hot for lunch.
